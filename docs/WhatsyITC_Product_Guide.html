AI behavior

Smart automation without losing control.

AI is used where it saves time. Human controls remain visible when trust, urgency, or business judgment matters.

What the AI uses

  • Selected agent instructions and tone.
  • Batch context and recipient details.
  • Recent WhatsApp message history.
  • Knowledge-base retrieval with embeddings.
  • Rules for human review and handoff.

What humans can control

  • Enable or disable AI per batch.
  • Choose the batch agent or use global default.
  • Edit or generate the next follow-up message.
  • Take over a conversation and send manually.
  • Return a resolved thread back to AI.

Recommended daily workflow

1
Open Human ReviewHandle critical and high-priority phone numbers first.
2
Check AI CRMReview batch-level momentum and action-required signals.
3
Inspect batchesOpen the batch control room only when a plan needs adjustment.
4
Improve KBAdd missing facts when AI confidence or answer quality drops.

Safety and reliability principles

The product avoids hidden automation. The UI shows send errors, paused follow-ups, handoff status, failed delivery, duplicate-phone warnings, and batch ownership so operators know why the AI did or did not act.

Visible errors Consent dialogs Batch ownership Manual override

Rollout checklist

How to get the best experience.

Keep the first rollout simple: one clean batch, one good agent, one strong knowledge base, and a daily review routine.

Before launch

  • Add WhatsApp credentials in Settings.
  • Create or verify message templates.
  • Add product and pricing knowledge.
  • Test agent replies in playground.

First batch

  • Upload a small clean spreadsheet.
  • Rename the batch clearly.
  • Approve and send initial template.
  • Enable AI only after checking recipients.

Daily operation

  • Start from Human Review.
  • Use AI help for suggested replies.
  • Watch AI CRM for batch trends.
  • Update knowledge when users ask new things.

Product promise

WhatsyITC turns scattered WhatsApp follow-ups into a focused operating system: every batch is trackable, every conversation is visible, AI handles routine replies, and humans see the numbers that actually need judgment.

Best first demo: upload a small batch, send one approved template, enable AI, send a customer reply from WhatsApp, then show the same phone appearing in Chats, AI Conversations, Human Review, and AI CRM.
